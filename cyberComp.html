The National Cyber League

  The National Cyber League allows the inclusion of anyone, regardless of experience, to join and learn about cybersecurity challenges that they will likely encounter in their careers. Challenges such as "...identifying hackers from forensic data, pentesting and auditing vulnerable websites, recovering from ransomware attacks, and much more!" This could be a valuable learning experience for those pursuing a career in cybersecurity!





Mt. SAC Won the Championship in 2012 and 3rd place in 2017!

Cyber Quests

  Cyber Quests are a series of online competitions that give students a chance to show off their knowledge in information security. Each quest tasks the student with analyzing a security system to locate any vulnerabilities. The quests are accessible since they range in difficulty from the level of a beginner to that of an experienced expert.
