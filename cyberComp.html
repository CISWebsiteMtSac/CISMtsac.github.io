The National Cyber Leage

  The National Cyber League allows the incusion of anyone, reguardless of experience, to join and to be able to learn about cyber security challenges that will likely happen in their careers, "such as identifying hackers from forensic data, pentesting and auditing vulnerable websites, recovering from ransomware attacks, and much more!" This experience will certainly help for anyone looking for cyber security careers!

Mt. SAC won the championship in 2012! Want to test yourself to bask in the glory of victory?

https://nationalcyberleague.org

Cyber Quests

  Cyber Quests are a series of online competitions that allows students to show their knowledge of information security, in which each quest requires the student to analyze a part of the security to see what is wrong by answering questions. The quests can range from identifying what is the most vulnerable to which is the most flawed part of the security. Naturally, the quests will be ranged for beginners with little to no experience, intermediate, to experienced experts knowledgable of cyber security.

https://uscc.cyberquests.org
