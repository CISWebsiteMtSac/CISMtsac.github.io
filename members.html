I graduated Spring of 2017 with my AS in Network Administration & Security Management.
I worked for the last 3.5 years as an IT Analyst for a Major Credit Bureau. I was working heavily with Cloud Technologies like AWS. I was very recently hired on to a public energy utility to work as an IT Associate rotating within 3 departments. I an currently working with the Networking and Architecture department. In this department I will be tasked with maintaining network data circuits and network appliances such as switches, routers, firewalls.
The Mt. SAC CIS department was key to preparing me for the IT jobs out there. Of course it is imperative that you learn new skills and technologies on your own by completing certificates or pursuing a higher education but the CIS department introduced me to people in IT with varying levels of experience. It was meeting those people that helped me get to where I am today.
