What is the Cis club?

  The CIS club is composed of students interested in the field of Information Systems and Information Technology. For many years the CIS club has fostered a higher level of learning through academic excellence and hands on experience in Information Systems. The CIS club promotes many campus activities and opportunities for community service, internships, and networking with many of today's top companies.

Join in Cyber Competitions!

  Want to put your cyber security skills to the test? Compete in cyber security based comptetitions, club members can assist you in signing up! This includes, but is not limited to the National Cyber League (NCL), where you can combat against hackers by protecting your data and figuring out how to put said hackers out of commission! There is also Cyber Quests, where you need to take on different challenges to identify flaws and solutions in a web server, think your cyber security knowledge and skills is up for the task? Even if you have no experience, you can join for fun! Click on the 'Cyber Competitions' tab for a complete overview, or if you want, check out either website with the links below!
